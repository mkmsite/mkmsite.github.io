Q-Learning Taxi Driver Simulator

  • Explore the world of reinforcement learning with a taxi AI simulator based on OpenAI's Taxi-v3 environment. Trained in a 5x5 grid world, our taxi AI learns how to efficiently pick-up and drop-off passengers.
  • Comprising of 500 unique states, the simulator combines the taxi's location, the passenger's location, and their intended destination to decide the best course of action. Six possible actions guide the taxi: navigating in four cardinal directions, picking up, and dropping off passengers.
  • The core code, built with robust Q-learning techniques, trains the agent over 1,000 episodes. This project showcases the effectiveness of reinforcement learning in dynamic environments.
  • Dive into the details and witness the AI in action here.

Pokémon Type Classifier

  • Embrace the enchanting realm of Pokémon with a machine learning twist. Harnessing logistic regression, this project unveils the complexities of predicting a Pokémon's primary type based on its attributes.
  • With a dataset teeming with captivating details from names to numerical strengths and weaknesses, the challenge isn't just to classify, but to deeply understand these virtual creatures.
  • Efficient preprocessing steps, from removing sparse categories to transforming categorical variables, ensure a smooth flow for model training. A meticulously constructed pipeline, consisting of data standardization and logistic regression, seamlessly classifies these captivating creatures.
  • Assessments through confusion matrices and in-depth metrics shed light on the model's precision and areas of improvement. Witness the blend of pop culture and machine learning here.
